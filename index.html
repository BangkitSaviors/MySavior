Abstract

MySavior is a capstone project developed by a talented team from Bangkit Academy to provide essential support and assistance for the elderly, particularly in emergencies. By leveraging advanced technologies, the platform ensures user safety, promotes health management, and strengthens the connection between elderly users and their guardians or caregivers.

Key features include:

  1. Shake Detection: An innovative feature that detects smartphone vibrations or shakes, automatically sending emergency alerts to designated contacts or caregivers.
  2. Health Monitoring Tools:
    • Manual BMI Calculation: Allows users to compute and monitor their Body Mass Index (BMI) for health optimization.
    • Blood Sugar Tracking: Enables users to manually input and track blood sugar levels for better health management.
  3. Live Location Sharing: Provides real-time location updates to guardians, ensuring swift assistance and reassurance during emergencies.
  4. Image Recognition for Food Analysis: Utilizes AI-powered image recognition to evaluate and classify food as healthy or unhealthy, promoting better dietary choices for elderly users.

This project harnesses cloud computing, machine learning, and mobile development to create an intuitive and impactful platform. MySavior represents the team’s commitment to improving the quality of life and safety for elderly users, making it a vital tool in promoting health and well-being in the aging population.
